Bio

Professor, Peking University.

Dr. Ping Wang (M’88–SM’16) is a professor and the director of intelligent Computing and Sensing Laboratory (iCSL) at Peking University, China. He is also a member of Key Laboratory of High Confidence Software Technologies (PKU), Ministry of Education, Beijing, China. His current research interests include IOT computing, System software, Information security and AI healthcare. He has authored or co-authored over 140 papers in venues such as WWW, AAAI, NeurIPS, USENIX Security, IEEE TDSC, IEEE TIFS, IEEE TKDE, ACM CCS, NDSS, Information Sciences and Ad Hoc Networks. Dr. Wang was a co-recipient of the Best Paper and two Outstanding Paper Awards from different professional societies.

Research interests include:

Privacy and Security, Data Mining, Cognitive Computing and Machine Learning.
